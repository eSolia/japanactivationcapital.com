A solution provided by Newton Investment Partners.

“Through constructive dialogue based on relationships of mutual trust as well as advisory and execution assistance, we will support Japanese companies with management teams that aspire to transform their businesses, improving the market expectations of companies’ continuous growth and achieving sustainable improvement of market capitalization.”

1.
Increase the number of listed companies that achieve high share price valuations by building sustainable growth expectations for higher multiples
We help companies transform into businesses that raise expectations for the future in order to increase the number of companies that can achieve higher valuations in a sustainable manner.
2.
Not only transforming our portfolio companies, but also positively impacting their related industries and the Japanese economy as a whole
By transforming our portfolio companies, we will make a positive impact on related industries that leads to higher growth for the Japanese economy.
3.
Fostering a virtuous economic cycle for the benefit of our environment and society
We will create a virtuous economic cycle and a sustainable economy for the benefit of the environment and our society by encouraging our portfolio companies to engage in activities that contribute to society and sustainability. Through the growth of our portfolio companies, profits will be distributed to both employees and SME business partners throughout the value chain, stimulating further investment into the economy.

Through mid- to long-term minority investments, we support companies with management teams that aspire to transform their businesses by helping them to create expectations of long-term and sustainable growth for their stakeholders.
  • We invest in leading companies listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.
  • Based on relationships of mutual trust with management teams that have the will to transform their companies, we will provide not only strategic advice but also support for the execution of strategic initiatives.
  • We support management teams in taking risks and implementing bold strategic initiatives that fundamentally and sustainably improve their company’s growth profile and create additional value for shareholders through improved multiple valuations. We do not propose actions like share buybacks that will only improve a company’s share price in the short term.
We were founded by an investment professional from a leading global private equity firm who has a strong track record of supporting the successful transformation of a number of Japanese companies. Together with senior professionals hailing from private equity firms, consulting firms, and investment banks, as well as senior executives from large corporations and financial institutions who have experience driving change, we intend to leverage our diverse knowledge base and broad network to provide high value-added solutions to portfolio companies.

Our board of directors and investment committee are comprised of experienced professionals with a variety of backgrounds, who can provide an outside perspective on strategy planning as well as management and investment decisions in order to maintain management objectivity.
